Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
💜

Overview

Aura AI Soulmate ("Aura", "the app", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is stored, and what is never collected or shared.

By using Aura, you agree to the practices described below.

🔒

Everything Stays on Your Device

Aura runs its AI model entirely on your phone. Your conversations are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party controlled by us.

We do not operate any backend servers. We have no ability to read your messages.
💬

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is saved in your phone's local storage only:

  • Chat messages — stored in a local SQLite database so your conversation history persists between sessions.
  • User facts — details you share (such as your name, age, job, location, hobbies, or mood) are saved in local app storage to help Aura remember you across sessions.
  • Session summary — a short one-sentence summary of recent conversations, refreshed periodically, stored locally to give Aura conversational context.

All of this data is stored in the app's private storage on your device. It is deleted when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

🚫

What We Do Not Collect

  • Your chat messages or conversation content
  • Your name, age, or any personal facts you share with Aura
  • Your location
  • Your device's contacts, camera, or microphone
  • Any analytics or usage telemetry from the app itself
📢

Advertising (AdMob)

Aura displays rewarded ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may collect certain data in connection with ad serving, including your device's advertising ID and general usage signals, in accordance with their own privacy policy.

View Google's Privacy Policy →

We do not receive or store any personal data from AdMob. Ad revenue helps keep the app free.

⬇️

Model Download

On first launch, Aura downloads the AI model file (~1.8 GB) from GitHub over the internet. No personal data is transmitted during this download — it is a standard file download, equivalent to downloading any app asset.

After the download, Aura does not require an internet connection to function (except to load ads).

👶

Children's Privacy

Aura is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has used this app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.
